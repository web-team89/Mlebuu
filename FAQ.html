Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is Mlebuu?

A: Mlebuu is a versatile online meeting application designed for both Android and PC users. It provides features such as chat, video calls, translation, raise hand, and unlimited meeting rooms.

Q: How do I create a meeting?

A: To create a meeting, simply click on the "Buat meeting" button on the homepage. You'll then have the option to start a new meeting or enter a meeting code.

Q: Are there any limitations on the free version?

A: The free version of Mlebuu offers unlimited meeting rooms, screen sharing, chatting, and raise hand feature. However, certain advanced features may be available in the premium version.

Q: How can I contact support?

A: If you need assistance, you can contact our support team by visiting the "Contact Us" page on our website. We are available to help you with any issues or questions you may have.

Q: Is Mlebuu compatible with mobile devices?

A: Yes, Mlebuu is compatible with both Android and iOS devices. You can download the mobile app from the respective app stores.

Q: Can I schedule meetings in advance?

A: Absolutely! Mlebuu allows you to schedule meetings in advance. You can set the date, time, and other preferences when creating a meeting.
